Welcome to the School of Computer Science at DiST

Empowering Innovators, Shaping the Future

Established in 2002, the School of Computer Science at De Paul Institute of Science & Technology (DiST) is dedicated to nurturing tech-savvy innovators ready to tackle tomorrow’s challenges. Our programs blend academic excellence with practical exposure, equipping students with the skills to thrive in the ever-evolving IT landscape.

With a curriculum designed to reflect current trends and advancements in IT, the department integrates theoretical studies with practical applications in state-of-the-art computer laboratories. A strong emphasis is placed on bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring students are prepared for real-world challenges.

The department is supported by a team of efficient, experienced, and dedicated faculty members who are committed to fostering both academic and interpersonal development. A variety of initiatives, including orientation programs, aptitude training, seminars, personality enhancement sessions, and mentoring programs, ensure the holistic development of students.

The School of Computer Science also emphasizes community engagement and social responsibility, encouraging students to participate in outreach activities and programs such as the National Service Scheme (NSS).

Mission

To ignite curiosity and inspire excellence in computing disciplines. We empower students with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills, shaping industry-ready professionals driven by innovation, ethical responsibility, and a passion for solving real-world challenges.

Quality Objectives
  • Intellectual, social, ethical, and spiritual excellence.
  • A strong impulse for innovation and entrepreneurial spirit.
  • A sense of social responsibility, inspiring them to become versatile and committed IT experts and leaders.
Why Choose Us?
  • Industry-Aligned Curriculum: Stay ahead with programs designed to reflect the latest tech trends.
  • Expert Faculty: Learn from dedicated educators committed to your growth.
  • Hands-On Learning: Bridge the gap between theory and practice in our state-of-the-art labs.
  • Holistic Development: From coding boot camps to personality enhancement sessions, we ensure you grow both academically and personally.
  • Community Impact: Make a difference through our social outreach initiatives.
Programmes Offered
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) (Honors)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA) – 2 years
  • Integrated Master of Computer Applications (MCA) – 5 years
