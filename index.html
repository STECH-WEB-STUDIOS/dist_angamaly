About DiST

De Paul Institute of Science & Technology (DiST), Angamaly, is an NAAC Accredited 'A' Grade with CGPA 3.11 (1st Cycle) and an ISO 9001:2015 Certified college run by De Paul Education Trust and owned by Marymatha Province of Vincentian Congregation. Education is one of the most important areas of its activities. The Vincentians hold aloft the sublime values of justice, peace, and love and aim at creating a new generation with a difference. The students must prove not only their academic excellence but also show a deep commitment to the society they live in. The institution strives to provide a holistic education that fosters intellectual, emotional, and social growth, preparing students to face the challenges of the modern world with confidence and integrity.
